Traditional Restaurant

A Taste of Albanian Heritage

Our restaurant at Vila Falo celebrates the rich culinary traditions of Albania, offering a menu filled with authentic dishes prepared using locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations.

Each meal is carefully crafted by our expert chefs who blend traditional techniques with modern presentations, creating a dining experience that honors our heritage while delighting contemporary palates.

How to Reach Us

Vila Falo is nestled in the picturesque mountains of Voskopoje, a historic village known for its natural beauty, fresh air, and cultural heritage. Located approximately 21 km from the city of Korçë in southeastern Albania, our resort offers the perfect blend of accessibility and seclusion.
